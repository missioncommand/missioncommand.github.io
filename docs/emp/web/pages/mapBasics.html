Map Basics

EMP3 supports drawing, editing, and displaying of basic shapes onto various maps

All map graphical interactions take place on overlays and not on the map itself.This allows for different overlays to display relevant or related features.

Overlays may be placed directly on to a map or nested within other overlays.

Overlays may exist as the children of multiple parent overlays

Features are the base graphical unit of EMP3 and will be used in this document to reference any kind of graphical feature on a map that is not background data or a map image service such as WMS

Supported Primitive Shapes

  • Circles
  • Ellipses
  • Paths
  • Points
  • Polygons
  • Rectangles
  • Squares
  • Text
